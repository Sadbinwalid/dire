Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025  ·  Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Dyer Realty NC ("we," "us," or "our"), operated by Butch Dyer as a licensed real estate professional affiliated with Keller Williams Realty, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you visit our website at dyerrealtync.com or interact with our real estate services.

Please read this policy carefully. If you do not agree with its terms, please discontinue use of our site.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address
  • Property address and details submitted via valuation or inquiry forms
  • Communication preferences and messages sent through contact forms
  • Account registration information if applicable

Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address and device identifiers
  • Browser type, operating system, and referring URLs
  • Pages visited, time spent on each page, and links clicked
  • Cookie data (see Section 5 below)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your real estate inquiries and provide requested services
  • Send market updates, property alerts, and newsletters (if you have opted in)
  • Conduct comparative market analyses and home valuations
  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations under North Carolina real estate law
  • Send transactional communications related to a pending or active transaction

We will not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share information with service providers who assist in operating our business (e.g., CRM software, email marketing platforms, IDX data providers) under appropriate data processing agreements.

3. NCREC Disclosure and Real Estate Communications

As a licensed real estate agent in North Carolina (NCREC), we are required to provide agency disclosures and may contact you for real estate purposes. By submitting a contact form or signing up for email communications, you consent to receive real estate-related communications. You may opt out at any time by clicking "unsubscribe" in any email or contacting us directly.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy and to comply with our legal obligations. Contact information from active client relationships is retained for a minimum of 7 years in compliance with North Carolina real estate record-keeping requirements.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. These may include:

  • Essential cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics and similar tools to understand site usage
  • Marketing cookies: Used for retargeting if you have visited specific property pages

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality.

6. Third-Party Services

Our site may integrate with third-party services including IDX property search feeds, Google Maps, social media platforms, and email marketing providers. These services have their own privacy policies and we encourage you to review them. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites linked from our website.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at butch@butchdyer.com or call 919-427-0069.

9. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of the website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact:
