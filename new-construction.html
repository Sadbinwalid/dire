The Critical Question

Why Bring Your Own Agent to a New Construction Purchase?

The builder's on-site sales agent works for the builder — not for you. Their job is to sell homes at the highest price, with the fewest concessions. Bringing your own agent (like Butch) costs you nothing and gives you someone in your corner from contract to closing.

Upgrade negotiation — Butch knows which builder upgrades are standard vs. overpriced. He'll steer you toward the ones with real ROI.
Contract review — Builder contracts favor builders. Butch reviews every clause so you know exactly what you're signing.
Inspection oversight — He'll attend every phase inspection and ensure the builder addresses punch-list items before closing.
Early lot access — Butch's long-standing builder relationships often mean first look at new communities before they're publicly advertised.

The builder pays the agent commission. Working with Butch on new construction costs you nothing extra — and can save you thousands in negotiations.
