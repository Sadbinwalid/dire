About This Community

The Reserve at Stonecroft is Wake Forest's newest master-planned community, offering thoughtfully designed homes that balance luxury and livability. Nestled among mature trees on the edge of the growing Wake Forest corridor, the community offers quick access to Highway 98, downtown Wake Forest's Main Street district, and top-rated Wake County schools.

Built by a partnership of regional builders with strong reputations for quality construction, Stonecroft offers flexible floor plans with options for first-floor primary suites, three-car garages, finished bonus rooms, and custom design studio selections.
