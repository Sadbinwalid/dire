Why It Matters

Online Estimates Are a Starting Point. Not an Answer.

Zestimate, Redfin Estimate, and similar tools work off public data — they don't know that your kitchen was professionally renovated, that there's a creek lot view, or that your street has lower traffic than the rest of the neighborhood.

Butch Dyer's comparative market analysis (CMA) is built on current sold data, active competition, neighborhood trends, and firsthand knowledge of what Triangle buyers are actually paying — and why.
